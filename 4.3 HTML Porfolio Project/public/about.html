About Me

Hello, I have been a nurse for 30 years and during Covid I stumbled upon an a low cost beginners coding boot camp. My wife and I started and completed it together and afterward I said to myself; "Hey, I think I really have something here."I also took up a great interest in statistics, math and science that I just did not have before. Who knew a global pandemic could bring out my inner Geek.

During the pandemic I also became painfully aware that I had no marketable skills to fall back on outside of nursing. This is something that I feel determined to remedy with a renewed zest for lifelong learning and skills acquisition. This discovery has liberated me from no longer feeling stuck in a rut.

Robert Green is oft quoted as saying:"A Jack of all trades is a master of none, but oftentimes better than a master of one". What I have taken from this quote, now seeing it with new eyes, is don't be content with only a superficial layer of knowledge on topics of interest but really deep dive as that is the only place that you find all of life's vast treasures. Henceforth, I am commiting myself to discovering all I can in the programming space. Blessings to you and yours.
